WORKING EXPERIENCE

Summer 2016 | TNO dept. Intelligent Imaging | Internship

During this internship I did my thesis research as part of a project called 'autmatic event search using semantics'. The goal of this project was to create a video search system which could detect high-level events such as 'Attempting a board trick' through low-level features such as colors, textures and shapes within the video frames.


Spring 2014 | MediaLab at Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences

Worked together with 4 other students on a 20 week project around the topic 'The Future of Television'. I had the role of researcher and concept developer within the team.


Fall 2013 | Internship at Mediamatic

Worked on the 'sensorproject'. The goal of this project was to monitor air quality using a small fine-dust sensor that would be bike-attachable. Users could monitor the air quality via a smartphone app. My role was to create a proof of concept of the sensor.
